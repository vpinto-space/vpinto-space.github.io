    Hello! Welcome to my personal website. My name is Victor Pinto and I'm a Ph.D. (c) in Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences (Space Physics) at at the University of California, Los Angeles.

    The main purpose of this site is to share some of my projects, publications and personal thoughts. While I plan to write mostly about work related stuff, I'll be probably posting a bit about everything that comes to my mind.

    Site is still under construction. Some parts are still not working, but I hope to get it fully functional soon.


    Research

    "Because to advance, we need science"

    The Earth's magnetic field interacts with the solar wind, protecting the planet from its direct impact. As a result, most of the energy is stored in both the field and the particles residing in it.

    Space Physics is the field of study that tries to understand the space environment by combining plasma theory, satellite data analysis and simulations. My current research interests are:

    • Solar wind-Magnetosphere Interactions
    • Radiation Belt Physics
    • Space Weather
